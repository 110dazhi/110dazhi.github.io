完成一个车道保持/变道之间的 损失函数

$\operatorname{cost}=1-e^{-\frac{\Delta d}{\Delta s}}$

Here, $\Delta d$ was the lateral distance between the goal lane and the final chosen lane, and $\Delta s$ was the longitudinal distance from the vehicle to the goal.
